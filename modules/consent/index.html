Reasoning Study

This study seeks to better understand how visualization can aid in reasoning tasks. Should you choose to participate you will be asked to:

  • Use a visualization and text to answer a question
  • Answer 4 surveys
  • Provide any feedback you wish
We expect the entire study to take about 15 minutes. You can find the full consent form below.

Form of Consent

You are invited to participate in a study conducted by the Visual Analytics Lab at Tufts University. Please read the following carefully before deciding to participate. You may participate in this study if you are between the ages of 18 and 65, are in the US, and are comfortable with communicating in English. We expect the entire study to take about 15 minutes maximum, but you will be allotted 60 minutes to complete the study. Your participation in the study is completely voluntary. If you choose to participate in this study you are free to revoke your consent and terminate the study at any time without penalties.

There are no direct benefits or foreseeable risks to you for participating in this study. However, if you decide to take part, you will play an integral role in the development of future visualizations. Any information collected for this study will be entirely confidential. The questionnaires that you would be required to complete will only be identifiable by an assigned ID number, and will not be associated with your name or any personally identifiable information.

Purpose of Study
We aim to better understand how visualizations can be used to aid in reasoning problems. The result of this study would help in the improvement of future visualization designs.

Task Description
You will be shown a visualization and text description of a reasoning problem and asked to answer two questions based on it. Please try to respond as accurately as possible. Afterwards, you will be asked to complete a task load questionnaire, a demographic questionnaire, a spatial ability questionnaire, and provide any additional feedack you wish.

You will be paid a base price of $0.80 and an additional $0.10 for every correct response. Total possible compensation will be $3.00.
